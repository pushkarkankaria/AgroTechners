The Decarboscrubber

Patented Apparatus.
Proven Process.

Feed Biomass

Bamboo, paddy, bagasse, or coconut shell fiber loaded into the input cylinder. Works regardless of moisture content. Feed size: 3mm to 8 feet.

Heating Cycle

De-carbonizer heats to 460°C over 3–5 hours using precision heating pads and rods. Smart sensor control. Two modes: Regular and Power Saving.

Gas Purification

Three-stage purifier — liquid scrubber → dry separator → refrigerated scrubber — cleans gases and captures dissolved micronutrients.

Collect Outputs

Liquid fertilizer, fuel gas (methane/hydrogen), and Carbon based Soil Sequister collected separately through dedicated output valves. Zero carbon emissions.

"In one operational cycle of 3 hours, 1 tonne of bamboo yields 200 litres of organic fertilizer, 230 kg of activated charcoal, and 130 kg of fuel gas — with zero carbon emissions."

International Protection Across 150+ Countries

The Decarboscrubber Apparatus is protected under the Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT), providing international patent protection across 150+ countries including India, USA, Europe, Japan, China, Canada, and all ARIPO, Eurasian, and OAPI member states.

The patent covers the full apparatus design, dual heating/cooling cycle, three-stage gas purification system, and the method for generating liquid fertilizer, gaseous fuel, and Carbon based Soil Sequister simultaneously from grass-type biomass.

About AgroTechners

The Minds Behind the Mission.

AgroTechners Biotech Pvt. Ltd. was founded by Shashank Balkrishna Joshi and Urmila Shashank Joshi in Dhule, Maharashtra. After years of independent R&D into biomass decarbonization, they developed and patented the Decarboscrubber.

Their fertilizer was tested on barren wasteland, which today grows trees and bears fruit. The mission is simple: restore India's degraded land, support farmers with organic inputs, and build a sustainable circular economy from biomass.

From a workshop in Dhule to international patent recognition under WIPO's PCT framework — AgroTechners represents the spirit of grassroots Indian innovation meeting global scientific standards.
